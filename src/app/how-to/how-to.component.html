What is the Pax Pokemon League Online (PPLO)?

PPLO is an online Pokémon League where you can battle Gym Leaders for prizes! In addition to Pokémon Battles, we have fun events planned during the weekend too! This event runs from July 14th-16th, 2023.

How to play!

Join our Discord! Register to play!

From the webapp, you can enter/exit Gym Leader queues to battle them and earn badges! While most things will be run through the webapp, but make sure to keep an eye on the Discord for announcements!

Prizes

Beat any 8 Gym Leaders and you can challenge the Champion! Win against the Champion and you have beaten the Pax Pokémon League Online 2023! You can see the list of prizes here!

Rules

Pretty much all major legendaries (plus Regieleki) are banned, while most minor legendaries and paradox 'mons are limited. We have a couple of other rules, too!
